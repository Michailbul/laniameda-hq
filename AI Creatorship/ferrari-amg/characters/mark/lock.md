# Mark — Identity Lock

> **The single source of truth for Mark across every shot.** Copy-paste the lock block below verbatim into every Nano Banana Pro and Seedance 2.0 prompt where Mark appears. Do not paraphrase. Do not summarize. Do not "improve" the wording.

## Who Mark Is

31. Best man at his childhood friend Michael's wedding. Driver of the red Ferrari 812 Superfast. On the surface: tailored, restrained, the "responsible adult" — the guy who shows up with the rings, drives a serious grown-up car, wears the three-piece tux to the letter. Underneath the costume he's still twelve years old. The platinum hoop in the left ear, the ornamental blackwork across the back of his right hand, the messy dirty-blonde tips at the crown — these are the tells. The grown-up outfit cannot quite contain the kid who races his friends through forests and eventually screams Limp Bizkit choruses at full tilt.

**Personality:** Controlled, slightly arrogant, loyal without saying it. Holds out for the first half of the film — jaw set, hands steady on the wheel, eyes forward. Then he breaks, and the kid comes back. Deep bond with Leo buried under composure. Would die before admitting how happy he is to see him.

## Physical Identity

- 31 years old, 182 cm, slim lean European build
- Short dirty-blonde to light warm-brown hair with sun-kissed lighter tips, naturally tousled and textured; soft pieces swept forward across the forehead, lift at the crown — lived-in, not styled
- Light blonde stubble, 1–2 days' growth, even across jaw and upper lip, not shaped
- Fair European skin with a visible scatter of light freckles across the bridge of the nose and upper cheeks
- Light green-hazel eyes, heavy-lidded, steady gaze
- Defined jawline, strong straight nose, moderately full lips
- Single small silver / platinum-tone hoop piercing in the **LEFT earlobe only**; right ear bare

## Wardrobe

Classic black three-piece tuxedo, tailored slim.

- **Jacket** — single-breasted, peak lapel with black satin facing, one-button closure, single back vent, worn fully open while driving
- **Waistcoat** — matching black wool with a deep U-shaped opening trimmed by a satin-faced shawl edge, a single covered button visible at the bottom (rest hidden by the jacket)
- **Shirt** — crisp pure white cotton tuxedo shirt, pleated bib front, spread collar, fastened down the bib with **three small round polished gold-tone studs**
- **Bow tie** — black silk, hand-tied, neat and level
- **Trousers** — black wool with matching black satin side stripe, flat front, tapered
- **Shoes** — polished **black patent-leather** oxford dress shoes, plain cap-toe, closed black lacing, **mirror high-shine** finish
- **Pocket square** — bright white silk in a flat presidential fold, peeking a fingernail above the jacket's breast pocket
- **Boutonnière** — single fresh white rose pinned to the left lapel

## Accessories & Marks

- **Watch** — vintage-style gold-tone round-case dress watch (~36 mm) with a warm gold dial, simple slim indices, and a small date window at 3 o'clock, mounted on a slim **dark brown leather strap**. Worn on the **LEFT wrist**.
- **Ear piercing** — single small platinum-tone hoop, **LEFT earlobe only**. Right ear bare.
- **Tattoo** — prominent ornamental geometric blackwork covering the entire back of the **RIGHT hand**. Central motif: stacked chevron/arrow triangles arranged around a vertical diamond, with solid dotwork-shaded geometric fills and fine parallel line "rays" radiating outward across the knuckles. Pure black ink on skin, no color. The tattoo **stops cleanly at the wrist** (never onto the forearm). The tattoo **does not extend onto the fingers** — back of the hand only. Fully visible whenever the right hand is in frame.
- **No rings** on any finger.
