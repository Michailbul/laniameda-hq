# Leo — Identity Lock

> **The single source of truth for Leo across every shot.** Copy-paste the lock block below verbatim into every Nano Banana Pro and Seedance 2.0 prompt where Leo appears. Do not paraphrase. Do not summarize. Do not "improve" the wording.

## Who Leo Is

32. Childhood friend of the groom. Driver of the matte charcoal Mercedes-AMG. **Not a groomsman, not a best man — no formal role at the wedding.** He's in the tux because Michael asked him to match. That's where his obligation ends. Leo showed up authentic — jacket thrown on the back seat, sleeves rolled, collar popped up, necktie knot loosened and sliding down an open shirt, suspenders on, white sneakers on his feet, oxfords dumped on top of the crumpled jacket in the back, a crushed boutonnière tossed onto the dashboard. The tuxedo is a costume he has already half-taken-off.

Where Mark dressed his identity down, Leo refused to. That is the whole emotional architecture of the film.

**Personality.** Loose, grinning, impossible to rattle. Competitive but never uptight. The guy who never stopped being twelve. Likes food, likes beer, likes speed, likes his friends too much to pretend otherwise. Drives the AMG like he already knows the punchline and is waiting for Mark to catch up. While Mark holds out, Leo is already rolling the window down. He is not a supermodel — solid ~90 kg frame, broader shoulders, softer edges, the body of a guy who enjoys his life. Life leaks out of him.

## Physical Identity

- 32 years old, 185 cm, **~90 kg — average broader European build, not ripped, not fat.** Mesomorph with a little softness. The body of a guy who enjoys himself: broader shoulders, slight fullness in the midsection, strong forearms. Reads as normal guy, never model.
- Medium-length **dirty-blonde to light warm-brown** hair, longer and messier than Mark's, natural shaggy layering with soft bangs falling across the forehead, slight lift at the crown — fully unstyled, looks slept-on
- **3–4 day dirty-blonde / light-brown stubble**, heavier than Mark's, even across jaw and upper lip, unshaped — not a full beard
- Fair European skin, lived-in warm tone, **no prominent freckles** (clear contrast vs Mark)
- **Light blue-grey eyes**, relaxed and amused, soft crinkle at the corners
- Straight nose, moderately full lips, **slightly fuller cheeks and a softer jawline** than Mark's sharp-cut face
- **No ear piercings** on either ear (clear contrast vs Mark's left hoop)

## Wardrobe — The Look

Classic black tuxedo, half-taken-off. Not a wardrobe of what he wears, but of what he no longer bothers wearing.

- **Jacket** — single-breasted, notch lapel with black satin facing. **NOT on his body.** Crumpled and thrown across the AMG's back seat as a messy pile. (Described fully in the AMG lock as an interior prop.)
- **Waistcoat** — NONE
- **Suspenders / braces** — **black formal suspenders**, thin to medium width, matte finish, button-attached to the trouser waistband, crossing over the shoulder blades at the back in a Y or H shape. Worn directly over the shirt, fully visible since there is no jacket or waistcoat covering them. These are Leo's visual anchor while driving — the straps framing the chest alongside the loose-hanging necktie.
- **Shirt** — crisp white cotton tuxedo shirt with pleated bib front, point collar. **Collar popped up at the back**, standing against the nape. **Top button at the collar UNDONE**, collar points slightly flaring open. **Sleeves rolled up neatly to just below the elbows** — sleeve cuffs bunched loose, French cuff undone and folded back. Plain dark shirt studs down the bib, no flash. Shirt partially untucked in places from driving.
- **Necktie** — **black silk necktie** (four-in-hand knot), not a bow tie. The knot is **loosened significantly** — pulled 4–5 inches down from the collar so it sits at mid-chest — with the top shirt button undone above it. The tie hangs straight down the center of the shirt bib between the two suspender straps. **Always worn loose in this film, never cinched tight.**
- **Trousers** — black wool with matching black satin side stripe, flat front, slim leg, held up by the suspenders (no belt).
- **Belt** — NONE (suspenders replace it)
- **Shoes on his feet** — crisp clean **white low-cut leather sneakers** with white rubber soles, minimal court silhouette, **no visible branding** (no swooshes, no stripes, no logos)
- **Dress shoes — NOT on his feet** — a pair of polished black calfskin oxfords sits **dumped on top of the crumpled jacket on the AMG's back seat**, laces flopped — messy pile, not neatly paired
- **Pocket square** — NONE
- **Boutonnière** — NOT pinned on Leo. A fresh white rose boutonnière lies **crushed on the dashboard** of the AMG as a visible prop

## Accessories & Marks

- **Watch** — rugged sport diver, brushed steel case ~41 mm, dark dial with simple hour markers, unidirectional bezel, **thick black rubber strap**. Worn on the **LEFT wrist**. Fully visible with sleeves rolled.
- **Ring** — single plain **matte black signet ring** on the **LEFT pinky** finger. No wedding ring. No other rings.
- **Bracelet** — thin black leather cord bracelet on the **right wrist**.
- **Tattoo** — **partial blackwork geometric sleeve on the RIGHT forearm, now fully visible because the sleeve is rolled up.** Fine line-pattern geometry starting at the back of the wrist and running up the forearm to just below the rolled sleeve, stopping above the elbow. Visible in every shot where the right arm is in frame. (Placement contrast vs Mark's back-of-hand tattoo — both men inked, different surfaces.)
- **Ear piercings** — NONE on either ear.
