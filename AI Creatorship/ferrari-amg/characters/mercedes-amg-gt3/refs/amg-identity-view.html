Prompt-Ready Description
A Mercedes-AMG GT3 race car in matte dark charcoal grey with a satin finish that absorbs light — no sharp reflections, only soft ambient gradients across the panels. Full GT3 spec with massively flared fenders exposing carbon fiber edges, a deep carbon front splitter with black mesh intake and dive planes, and a large carbon rear diffuser with vertical fins housing center-mounted dual round exhaust tips. A massive full-width carbon fiber rear wing with visible weave pattern sits on angular swan-neck pylons, carrying a large red AMG striped emblem on the wing's rear face. Front face: Panamericana vertical slat grille with a three-pointed star at center, slim LED headlights with bright white DRL strips, small German flag tri-stripe on the upper left hood edge. Rear face: center high-mount LED brake strip, two wide horizontal taillights glowing red-orange, small Mercedes star emblem on the trunk, two yellow Pirelli rectangles on the upper bumper, two red Motul rectangles on the lower bumper corners. Driver side (left) carries only two elements: a vertical German flag stripe (black-yellow-red) on the rear pillar from roofline to skirt, and a small AMG striped emblem with "AMG" text in subtle grey-white on the lower door near the side skirt. Passenger side (right) is completely clean — no text, no logos, no decals. Matte black mesh-pattern racing wheels with silver center-lock nuts, large red AMG brake calipers visible through the mesh, Pirelli slick tires. Carbon mirrors with red accent tips. Thin black roof antenna. No license plates. No race number anywhere.
Rules
This is a RACE CAR. Wing, splitter, diffuser, wide body, carbon elements are mandatory. Never simplify to "an AMG GT."
Wing branding is RED AMG on the WING ELEMENT itself (carbon surface), not on the rear trunk deck.
Taillights are horizontal LED strips, NOT round.
Exhaust is CENTER through the diffuser, NOT side-exit.
Pirelli = yellow rectangles. Motul = red rectangles.
Matte finish vs Ferrari's gloss = core visual contrast of this project.
No plates. No race number. Explicitly negate both in every prompt.
