# Guardrail Squirrel Reaction

Use with the existing guardrail squirrel start frame. The start frame supplies the animal, guardrail, road, forest, and light. The prompt only directs the reaction.

## v1 - alert stare

```text
Same squirrel throughout the shot, same body shape and fur pattern. The squirrel is already upright on the guardrail and suddenly becomes alert, freezing for a beat as distant vibration reaches it. Locked tripod camera, no camera movement, shallow depth of field. The squirrel slowly turns its head toward camera-left, ears rotating forward, then gives one sharp tail flick while the background forest remains soft and still. Fine fur movement, tiny chest breathing, subtle guardrail vibration, warm backlit haze. Natural wildlife documentary realism, 24fps, no extra animals, no cars entering frame.
```

## v2 - grooming interruption

```text
Same squirrel throughout the shot, same body shape and fur pattern. The squirrel starts grooming its face with its front paws, then abruptly stops mid-gesture as the guardrail vibrates from an approaching off-screen force. Locked tripod camera, no camera movement, shallow depth of field. The squirrel lifts its head, ears snap forward, eyes fixed down the road, tail bristling slightly. Keep the movement small and realistic: paw motion, ear rotation, chest breathing, one tail flick. Warm backlit haze, forest background soft. No cars visible, no camera drift, no extra animals.
```
