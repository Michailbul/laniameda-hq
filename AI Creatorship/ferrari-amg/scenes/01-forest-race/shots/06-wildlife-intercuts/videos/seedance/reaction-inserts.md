# Wildlife Reaction Inserts

## v1 - puddle shockwave

```text
Macro close-up of a still puddle on the forest road shoulder, no cars visible. Reflected tree trunks and warm sky sit on the water surface. At second zero the puddle begins to vibrate from distant engine force transmitted through the ground. Concentric ripples spread outward, the reflection fractures into moving shards, a single leaf trembles at the edge and drops into the water. Locked-off camera, no camera movement, shallow depth of field, realistic water surface tension, tiny debris floating, warm backlit haze in the background. Quiet cinematic realism, no animals, no vehicles entering frame.
```

## v2 - leaves vortex

```text
Road-level static shot across the damp asphalt shoulder, no cars visible. Fallen leaves and small debris lie still in the foreground. At second zero a pressure wave from a just-passed race car hits the frame: leaves rip upward, spiral into a tight vortex, hang in the warm backlit haze for a beat, then scatter toward the forest floor. Locked camera, sharp road texture, visible air wake through leaf motion, droplets flicking from the asphalt edge, teal shadows and amber backlight. No vehicles enter frame, only the physical aftermath of speed.
```

Leaves shake from their departure and the branch rebounds with realistic spring motion. Locked camera, shallow depth of field, warm backlit haze through the trees, no cars visible. Keep the action short and readable: head snap, wing burst, branch sway, leaves settling.
