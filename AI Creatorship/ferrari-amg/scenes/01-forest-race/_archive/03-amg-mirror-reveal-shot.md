# Shot 03 - AMG Mirror Reveal

## Purpose

Introduce the AMG as a threat arriving, not as an equal already in the race. The ideal version is a Ferrari mirror reflection. The guaranteed fallback is the AMG emerging from haze behind the Ferrari.

## Preferred Version

Close-up on Ferrari side mirror or rearview mirror. AMG headlights grow inside the reflection. Mirror vibrates from speed.

## Safe Version

Rear tracking on the Ferrari. Deep in the haze behind it, the AMG shape and headlights appear and begin closing.

## Failure Watch

- Reflection rendering may fail. Do not spend the whole session on it.
- AMG identity can be partial here: dark shape, wing silhouette, white headlights are enough.
- Full AMG reveal belongs in Shot 04.
