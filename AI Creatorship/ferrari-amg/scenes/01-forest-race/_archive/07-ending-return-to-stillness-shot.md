# Shot 07 - Ending Return to Stillness

## Purpose

Close the loop. The cars are gone. The forest settles. The squirrel or the empty road returns the film from spectacle to story.

## Ideal Ending

Two-shot ending:

1. Long road with taillights vanishing into haze.
2. Guardrail callback: empty road, then squirrel returns or sits quietly.

## Camera

Static, slower, quieter. Let the frame breathe.

## Failure Watch

- Squirrel climbing into frame may break. Start with squirrel already present if needed.
- The ending should be simple. No style switch, no chase action.
