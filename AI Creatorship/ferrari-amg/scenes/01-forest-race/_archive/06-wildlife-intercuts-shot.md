# Shot 06 - Wildlife Intercuts

## Purpose

Short forest reaction beats between car shots. These are not cute animal shots. They are punctuation: vibration, fear, air wake, water ripple, branch movement.

## Best First Batch

Generate or animate:

- puddle shockwave
- leaves vortex
- birds scatter
- squirrel bolt as a callback if Shot 01 works

## Edit Rule

Never put two wildlife cuts back-to-back. Use them between car shots.

## Failure Watch

- Animal anatomy can fail if the movement is too long.
- These should be 1-2 seconds in the final edit.
- The car does not need to be visible. The reaction is enough.
