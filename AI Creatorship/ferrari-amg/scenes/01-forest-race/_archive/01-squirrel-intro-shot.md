# Shot 01 - Squirrel Intro

## Purpose

Open the film with quiet forest life before the machines arrive. The squirrel is the audience surrogate: calm, alert, then disrupted.

## Existing Asset

```text
scenes/01-forest-race/freepik_a-red-squirrel-sitting-up_2790306043.png
```

Red squirrel on a metal guardrail, forest road soft behind it, haze and god rays. Strong start-frame candidate.

## Best First Attempt

Use the existing guardrail squirrel image. Keep the camera locked. Animate one small reaction chain: grooming or stillness -> freeze -> head turn -> tail flick. Avoid complex animal movement until Seedance proves it can hold the squirrel anatomy.

## Camera

Static locked tripod, shallow depth of field, medium close-up. The still camera creates contrast with the tracking shots that follow.

## Failure Watch

- Paw grooming may break.
- Squirrel can morph if action is too complex.
- Seedance may add camera drift unless told "locked tripod, no camera movement."
