# Shot 02 - Ferrari Continuous Tracking

## Purpose

Introduce the Ferrari alone before the AMG appears. This is the technical calibration shot for chained Seedance clips: generate clip A, extract the final frame, use it as the start frame for clip B, and stitch in edit.

## Existing Asset

```text
scenes/01-forest-race/ferari back lone .png
```

Ferrari rear solo, centered, haze, god rays. Use this first. Do not add AMG.

## Action

Rear tracking pursuit. The Ferrari holds frame as the forest streaks past. Clip 1 handles the curve. Clip 2 continues from the extracted last frame and lets the Ferrari accelerate slightly away.

## Camera

Low rear tracking at road level, speed-matched, no height change, no lateral drift for the first calibration run.

## Failure Watch

- Ferrari identity can drift at seams.
- Road direction can jump if clip 2 re-describes the scene instead of continuing from the start frame.
- If the camera changes height or angle, the stitch becomes visible.
