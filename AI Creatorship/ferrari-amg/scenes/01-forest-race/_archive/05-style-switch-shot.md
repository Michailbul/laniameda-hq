# Shot 05 - Style Switch

## Purpose

One deliberate AI-native rupture. This is not a glitch. It is the flex.

## Best Base Shot

Use a simple tracking shot: Ferrari solo or both cars rear tracking on a straight road. Do not test the rupture on a complex overtake or mirror shot.

## Preferred Direction

Rain storm transformation first. It stays photoreal but changes the world. White-marker outline flash is the backup because it can be done through the frame-vfx-stylizer pipeline if Seedance does not hold it.

## Failure Watch

- Do not combine rain shift and sketch shift in one generation.
- The style switch should happen once.
- Snap back to photorealism hard. Do not fade if cutting in post.
