# AI Style Rupture

## Thesis

Use one deliberate visual rupture as the "AI can do this" moment. It should feel intentional, not like generation failure. The strongest options are a sudden rain transformation or a white-marker outline flash applied to a stable chase shot.

## Visual Grammar

- Base shot stays simple: rear tracking, straight road, both cars or Ferrari solo.
- Rupture happens once, clearly, then snaps back.
- Subject identity stays stable through the rupture.
- Physics must carry the transformation: rain beads, spray, wake, leaves, road reflections, outline persistence.

## Use When

- The edit already has enough grounded car footage.
- We need one impossible beat for social impact.
- The shot can tolerate post-production compositing if pure Seedance fails.

## Avoid

- Multiple style switches in a row.
- Mixing weather shift and rendering-style shift in the same prompt.
- Rupturing a complex overtake or mirror reflection shot.
