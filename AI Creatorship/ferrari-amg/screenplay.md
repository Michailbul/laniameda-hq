# FERRARI + AMG — "ROLLIN'"

*A short film. Approx. 3 minutes. Dir. Michael / Laniameda.*

---

## Logline

Two childhood friends in tuxedos, en route to the wedding of a third, race their dream cars through a golden-hour forest and remember they're still twelve years old.

## Theme

You're never too old to be a kid. Past reclaims present.

## Tone

Tense cinematic open → absurd humor mid → euphoric release. European automotive realism with the soul of a coming-of-age short.

## Continuity Locks

- **Ferrari 812 Superfast** — red metallic, glossy. No plates. Warm visual anchor.
- **Mercedes-AMG GT R** — matte dark charcoal grey (road-legal GT R, not GT3). No plates. Darker threat presence.
- **Mark** (31) — three-piece tux, platinum ear piercing (left), tattoo edge visible under right cuff.
- **Leo** (32) — tux with bow tie undone around his neck, stubble, driving in white sneakers, dress shoes on passenger seat, crushed rose boutonnière on dash.
- **Michael** — the groom. Childhood friend of both Mark and Leo. Seen only in Act III.
- **Mark is the best man.** Ring box wedged in the Ferrari's armrest cavity.
- **Location** — European forest road, autumn, late golden hour. Damp reflective asphalt, god rays through canopy, haze always present.

## Structure

- **Act I — The Forest** (~00:45) — Shots 1–6
- **Act II — The Race** (~01:45) — Shots 7–13
- **Act III — The Arrival** (~00:30) — Shots 14–15

## ACT I — THE FOREST *(~00:00 – 00:45)*

---

### SHOT 1 — EXT. FOREST CANOPY — GOLDEN HOUR
*Close-up, static, long lens.*

A red squirrel sits on a pine branch high above the road, cheeks full of acorn. Through the negative space of the canopy below her, a two-lane forest road threads like a ribbon. Dust motes drift in slanted god rays. The forest is breathing.

**OS — a distant, low-frequency engine roar.** 812 Superfast. V12. Unmistakable.

The squirrel freezes. Her paws loosen. The acorn slips and tumbles down through the branches.

---

### SHOT 2 — EXT. TREE — SQUIRREL POV / TRACKING DESCENT
*Handheld, following, shallow DOF.*

Camera descends with the squirrel as she scrambles down the trunk. Bark, moss, leaves strobing past. Sunlight flickers through branches in rhythm with her panic.

Camera clears the lowest branch. Reveals the **ROAD.**

---

### SHOT 3 — EXT. FOREST ROAD — LOW TRACKING PASS-BY
*Ground-level, 50mm, into-sun.*

The **FERRARI 812 SUPERFAST** — red metallic — tears into frame, backlit, leaf debris exploding in its wake. The forest swallows it in two seconds. Engine note dopplers out.

Mist reclaims the road.

---

### SHOT 4 — INT. FERRARI 812 — DRIVING
*Interior coverage, three beats in one shot.*

Close on **MARK** (31). Tux. Jaw tight. A single platinum piercing in his left ear. Under his right cuff, the edge of a tattoo — half-covered by his watch.

Rack focus → center console: a **black ring box** wedged into the armrest cavity. Wedding **boutonnière** beside it in florist plastic.

Mark flips the **SUN VISOR** down. Camera rotates 180° with the mirror — the visor mirror now reveals his eyes in close-up, and tucked behind the elastic strap: a **wedding invitation,** gold foil.

He reads it. Snaps the visor shut.

The dash-phone rings.

---

### SHOT 5 — INT. FERRARI 812 — PHONE CALL
*Continuous, same lens, intercut with LEO when needed.*

Mark thumbs the wheel button.

> **LEO (V.O.)**
> Still driving that overpriced hairdryer, don't ya?
>
> **MARK**
> Where the hell are you?
>
> **LEO (V.O.)**
> Right behind ya.

Mark's eyes flick to the rearview.

**INSERT — REARVIEW MIRROR.** The matte charcoal **AMG GT R** fills the glass. It has been there the whole time.

> **LEO (V.O.)**
> Michael's losing his mind. You got the rings?
>
> **MARK**
> *(taps the ring box in the armrest)*
> Got his rings.
>
> **LEO (V.O.)**
> Best man shows up last, huh?
>
> **MARK**
> *(small smile)*
> See you there.
>
> **LEO (V.O.)**
> Not if I see you first.

Line dies.

---

### SHOT 6 — INT. AMG GT R — DRIVING
*Interior, handheld energy, push in.*

**LEO** (32). Bow tie hanging loose around his neck. Stubble. A fresh rose boutonnière lies crushed on the dash. He's driving in **white sneakers.** Polished black dress shoes sit on the passenger seat.

He grins. Drops a gear.

**MUSIC CUE — "ROLLIN'" — drops hard on the downbeat.**

---

## ACT II — THE RACE *(~00:45 – 02:30)*

---

### SHOT 7 — EXT. FOREST ROAD — LOW WIDE PASS-BY
*Ground-level, 35mm, into-sun, slow-motion resolving to real-time on downbeat.*

The Ferrari and AMG explode past camera side by side, backlit, kicking golden leaf debris at the lens. The engines harmonize with the downbeat of the riff. Mist tears.

---

### SHOT 8 — INT. FERRARI / INT. AMG — CROSSCUT
*Matched close-ups. Both interiors. 50mm. No lyrics yet — song is on verse/pre-chorus.*

CU **MARK** — jaw locked, not smiling, racing against his will.

CUT — CU **LEO** — head rocking with the beat, one hand on the wheel. He glances right through his side window.

LEO'S POV through both windshields: Mark staring dead ahead, refusing to look.

Leo lets out a soundless "pff, whatever" smile. Drops a gear.

---

### SHOT 9 — EXT. FOREST — HIGH CRANE / AERIAL
*Sweeping descent through canopy.*

Both cars thread the forest road at full speed. Canopy rips past. Golden hour spearing through gaps. The forest is the scale against which the speed finally reads.

---

### SHOT 10 — EXT. PINE BRANCH — SQUIRREL CAMEO
*Tight close-up, static, comedic timing. The tonal pivot of the film.*

Our squirrel is back. Eye-line down to the road. In the out-of-focus bokeh below her, streaks of red and charcoal rip past with the engine roar. She holds.

She slowly shakes her head. Raises one front paw to her temple. Makes a tiny **finger-twirl** next to her head — the universal "they're nuts." Lowers the paw. Holds a beat. Deadpan to camera.

*Production note: if the finger-twirl doesn't render cleanly in Seedance, fall back to (a) paw-to-forehead facepalm with head droop, (b) paw covering her eyes, or (c) slow head-shake only. Generate all variants and keep the cleanest read.*

---

### SHOT 11 — INT. FERRARI / INT. AMG — CHORUS PEAK CROSSCUT
*The emotional climax of the film. Alternating CUs in rhythm with the chorus.*

Both men, full chorus. **Mark is now singing** — first time in the film. Leo is **howling** along, fist hitting the headliner on every beat. They glance at each other through both windshields at the same moment —

— same grin. Twelve-year-olds in tuxes.

> *"Keep rollin', rollin', rollin', rollin'..."*

*The conversion happened between Shot 10 and Shot 11. Audience never sees the moment Mark cracks. The squirrel's judgment was the pivot.*

---

### SHOT 12 — EXT. FOREST ROAD — LOW WIDE TWO-SHOT
*Ground-level, 24mm, into-sun.*

Ferrari and AMG side by side at full tilt, wing-mirror to wing-mirror. The forest is a tunnel of gold and backlight haze around them.

---

### SHOT 13 — EXT. FOREST RISE — LOW WIDE, DUTCH, CREST SHOT
*Ground level, 24mm, into-sun.*

Both cars go airborne over a rise, wing-mirror to wing-mirror. Suspension unloading. Golden hour lancing through mist and leaf debris. The cars land in unison. Tires bark. They disappear down the next bend.

---

## ACT III — THE ARRIVAL *(~02:30 – 03:00)*

---

### SHOT 14 — EXT. WEDDING FIELD — EXTREME WIDE, CRANE DOWN TO MS
*One continuous move: aerial reveal descending into the cars parking.*

Camera reveals a **CATHEDRAL OF WHITE TENTS** in a forest clearing — Goblet-of-Fire scale. Lanterns strung between trees. Formalwear guests dot the field. Two distant streaks — red and charcoal — carve out of the tree line toward it.

Camera descends. Cars roll up in a double cloud of dust. Park side by side. Engines tick.

Doors open. **MARK** steps out, brushing forest dust off his lapel. Glances at his watch.

> **MARK**
> Damn. We're still late.

**LEO** climbs out the other side laughing, grabs his dress shoes off the passenger seat and hops on one foot pulling them on.

> **LEO**
> *(grinning)*
> At least neither of us showed up in a Tesla.

**MICHAEL** (the groom) jogs in from the tents, arms wide, shaking his head. All three laugh.

---

### SHOT 15 — EXT. WEDDING CAMP — THESIS SHOT / CRANE UP
*One continuous move: MS dolly-in on the hug, pulling up and out into the sky.*

Leo throws his arm around Mark's neck. **Mark is laughing.** The serious face from Act I is gone entirely. Two boys in tuxes, covered in forest dust, walking away from camera toward the tents.

Camera cranes up and back. The wedding camp opens out below — tents, lanterns, guests, two toy-sized cars parked at the edge of the forest. Golden hour floods the lens.

The song fades into its outro. Strings.

**FADE TO BLACK.**

**END.**

## Production Notes

- **Music cue is the skeleton.** "Rollin'" is non-diegetic. It drops on Leo's gear change in Shot 6. Chorus lands on Shot 11 (Mark joining). Outro carries under Shots 14–15.
- **No dialogue during the race** except the mouthed/sung chorus. Silence and music carry the character arc.
- **Identity locks** carry through every in-car shot. Ferrari red metallic. AMG matte charcoal GT R. Mark's piercing + tattoo-under-cuff. Leo's undone bow tie + sneakers.
- **Each act has one "feature" beat:** Act I = the visor reveal (Shot 4). Act II = the squirrel pivot (Shot 10). Act III = the thesis laugh (Shot 15).
- **Mark's arc happens in the cut** between Shot 10 and Shot 11. The audience fills in the conversion. Don't show it — imply it.
- **Squirrel is a callback, not a gag.** She's scared in Shot 1, judging in Shot 10. She's grown up through the film in reverse — the boys have regressed, she has matured.
