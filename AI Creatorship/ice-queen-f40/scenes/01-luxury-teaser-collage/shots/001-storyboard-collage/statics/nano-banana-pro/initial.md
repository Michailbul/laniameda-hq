# Initial Storyboard Collage

Main Nano Banana Pro storyboard prompt for the Chinese Ferrari perfume brief.

## v1

Structured for Nano Banana Pro. Unlabeled 12-panel grid, anonymous perfume object, locked woman/F40 direction, red elevator and gray Tokyo split.

```
GOAL:
Create one image: an unlabeled 12-panel luxury teaser storyboard collage for a Chinese Ferrari perfume film. The image should feel like a high-end perfume campaign contact sheet crossed with an automotive fashion film.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
One complete collage image arranged as exactly 12 separate image panels in a clean 4 columns x 3 rows grid.
Use thin clean gutters between panels.
The panels must be completely unlabeled: no panel numbers, no captions, no headings, no corner labels, no timeline marks.
No readable text anywhere in the image.
Every panel must look like a still from the same expensive teaser film.

CORE SUBJECT LOCKS:
Same 22-year-old East Asian woman throughout — long straight jet-black hair center-parted with face-framing strands across cheek and shoulders, porcelain cool-toned matte skin, sharp cheekbones, strong jaw, full lips in soft rose-nude stain, dark almond eyes with long lashes, straight dark brows with minimal arch, cold aloof chin-lifted expression. Signature wardrobe: matte black high-neck stretch bodysuit with deep center-front V-split revealing sternum, integrated long black opera gloves extending from the sleeves, sculptural molten-silver chest clasp with liquid-metal chain detail, asymmetric liquid-silver drip earrings in dripping chrome forms, long pointed metallic-black talon nails, black satin wide-leg trousers with ankle slit, pointed patent-black stiletto pumps. Zero identity drift.

Same 1987 Ferrari F40 throughout — deep gloss-black lacquer paint, dark five-spoke alloy wheels in matte dark bronze finish, signature slatted rear decklid, pop-up headlights, NACA side ducts, large fixed rear wing, triple center-exit exhaust, Japanese license plate, period-authentic 1987 silhouette. Interior: exposed chrome-gate manual shifter with six-slot gate pattern and round black shift knob, keyed ignition (not push-start), round Veglia analog gauges with red tachometer needle, carbon-fiber dash panels with visible weave, lightweight black fabric racing bucket seats, no modern electronics or screens.

PRODUCT LOCK:
Anonymous minimalist perfume bottle, small matte-black sculptural glass body, no visible label, no logo, no readable text, brushed-silver atomizer cap, refined and expensive. The bottle should feel like a ritual object, not a commercial packshot.

WORLD SPLIT:
Red mode panels happen only inside a private polished black-lacquer elevator: deep blood-red atmospheric light wash, warm tungsten key from high camera-right, cool blue fill from camera-left, crushed black shadows under jaw, subtle red LED accent near ceiling, no visible buttons or signage.
Gray mode panels happen only in urban Tokyo daylight near or inside the black F40: overcast soft diffused sky, cool desaturated gray concrete and paving stone, yellow tactile paving along the curb, contemporary mid-rise glass architecture in soft focus, empty street, no rain, no neon, no night.

UNLABELED PANEL SEQUENCE - EXACTLY THESE 12 PANELS:
Panel 1: Product/title anchor with no text: anonymous matte-black perfume bottle with brushed-silver atomizer cap centered like a sculptural object on glossy black lacquer; soft red elevator glow; the woman's silver chest clasp and a distorted reflection of the black F40 implied in the chrome cap.
Panel 2: Red elevator full-body fashion frame: the woman mid-turn inside the polished black-lacquer elevator, hair moving across cheek, chin lifted, silver chest clasp catching warm red light.
Panel 3: Gray Tokyo automotive frame: the gloss-black 1987 Ferrari F40 parked or gently rolling on a quiet Tokyo side street, three-quarter front angle, empty street, overcast daylight.
Panel 4: Macro material insert: molten-silver chest clasp and liquid-metal chain across black fabric, warm red elevator light, shallow depth of field.
Panel 5: Perfume still life: anonymous matte-black perfume bottle on glossy black lacquer with mirror reflection, silver atomizer catching a sharp highlight, no label.
Panel 6: F40 detail insert: close-up of pop-up headlight, wheel, NACA duct, or chrome-gate shifter with black lacquer/carbon texture, cool gray daylight.
Panel 7: Perfume ritual: the woman sprays perfume once at the side of her throat with a black-gloved hand, fine mist visible in warm red light, controlled expression.
Panel 8: Abstract motion: jet-black hair or black fabric moving with precision through red light, one clean direction of motion, silver earring or chain briefly catching highlight.
Panel 9: Product sculpture: perfume bottle, silver drip earrings, black talon nails, and chrome accessory fragments arranged like a small luxury sculpture on black lacquer.
Panel 10: Reflection frame: the woman's face or gloved hand reflected in gloss-black F40 paint or polished elevator lacquer, distorted but elegant, silver detail sharp.
Panel 11: Dynamic passage: woman walking past the gloss-black F40 on gray Tokyo pavement, slight cinematic motion blur, chin lifted, gaze forward, car parallel behind her.
Panel 12: Final tableau: woman and black Ferrari F40 held still in the far distance on a quiet gray Tokyo street, almost untouchable, fading into cool overcast daylight.

VISUAL STYLE:
High-end perfume advertising meets automotive editorial cinema.
Cold, controlled, ritualistic, expensive.
Black, deep red, cool gray, chrome, silver, and gloss-black lacquer.
Strong emphasis on material texture: black lacquer, matte black glass, chrome atomizer, silver jewelry, carbon fiber, F40 paint, glove tension, perfume mist, hair motion.

CAMERA:
Medium-format fashion photography and cinema hybrid.
Sharp focus where intended, controlled depth of field, refined lens compression, no noise.
Mix wide full-body frames, macro material inserts, product still lifes, automotive details, and close cinematic reflections.

NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS:
No watermark.
No panel numbers.
No captions.
No labels.
No subtitles.
No readable text anywhere.
No external brand logos or signage.
No panther.
No pure white studio void.
No neon cyberpunk.
No rain, no night, no crowd, no pedestrians.
No cigarette.
Do not sexualize the woman. Keep the styling luxury-fashion, controlled, and editorial.
Do not turn the F40 into a modern supercar. Keep the 1987 Ferrari F40 silhouette period-correct.

VALIDATION:
Before finalizing, check that the collage has exactly 12 distinct unlabeled panels in a 4x3 grid, no readable text anywhere, the same woman appears consistently, the same black Ferrari F40 appears consistently, the perfume bottle is anonymous and unlabeled, red mode stays in the elevator, and gray mode stays in Tokyo daylight.
```
