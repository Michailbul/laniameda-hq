# Luxury Teaser Collage

## Description

Twelve-panel non-linear storyboard for the Chinese Ferrari perfume direction: red elevator perfume ritual, gloss-black F40, gray Tokyo daylight, chrome and black lacquer details.

## Storyboard

1. Anonymous perfume object as the title/product anchor, with woman and F40 implied through reflection.
2. Woman mid-turn in the red elevator.
3. Gloss-black F40 on gray Tokyo street.
4. Molten-silver clasp and chain macro.
5. Anonymous matte-black perfume bottle on black lacquer.
6. F40 headlight or wheel detail in gray daylight.
7. Perfume spray at the side of her throat.
8. Hair or black fabric motion in red light.
9. Silver accessories and bottle as sculptural still life.
10. Woman reflected in F40 paint or elevator lacquer.
11. Woman walking past F40 with slight motion blur.
12. Final distant tableau with woman and F40 held still in gray Tokyo daylight.
