# Chinese Ferrari Perfume

## Thesis

A withheld perfume film where a locked East Asian woman, a gloss-black 1987 Ferrari F40, and a minimalist anonymous perfume bottle become one cold luxury system. Red elevator ritual cuts against gray Tokyo automotive stillness.

## Visual Grammar

- Red mode: deep blood-red elevator, black lacquer, warm tungsten key, cool rim, crushed jaw shadows, skin and perfume mist.
- Gray mode: overcast Tokyo daylight, cool concrete, yellow tactile paving, glass architecture, gloss-black F40, low automotive stance.
- Metallic language: molten-silver chest clasp, silver drip earrings, chrome atomizer, chrome-gate shifter, black lacquer paint.
- Camera rhythm: macro obsession, hard jump cuts, quiet full-body distance, held silhouettes.

## Use When

- The shot should feel like a perfume ad with a car obsession.
- The woman should feel controlled and untouchable without slipping into generic villain styling.
- The F40 should feel like a scent note: black lacquer, carbon, chrome, gasoline memory, Tokyo gray.

## Avoid

- Panther imagery.
- Pure white studio void.
- Y2K chrome collage detached from the F40 world.
- External brand labels or readable product text.
- Neon cyberpunk.
