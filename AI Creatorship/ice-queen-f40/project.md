# Chinese Ferrari Perfume

## Brief

Cinematic fashion/automotive perfume film built around a locked East Asian woman, a gloss-black 1987 Ferrari F40, and an anonymous matte-black perfume bottle with silver atomizer hardware.

The current creative lane is Chinese Ferrari perfume: cold, controlled, expensive, ritualistic, and built from two worlds:

- red elevator B-roll for perfume ritual, skin, jewelry, hair, glove, mist, and silhouette
- gray Tokyo daylight exterior for the F40, pavement, street movement, and automotive power

## Direction

Perfume-ad cadence with automotive obsession. No generic luxury montage. The woman, the F40, and the perfume bottle carry the brand. Camera attention lands on the silver chest clasp, silver drip earrings, talon nails, black lacquer, chrome-gate shifter, perfume mist, F40 paint, and Tokyo gray.

## Output

15-20s final, stitched in post from a library of 4-8s Seedance clips.

## Pipeline

Stills with Nano Banana Pro and Midjourney. Lock winners as start frames. Animate in Seedance V2. Cherry-pick. Stitch in post.

## Constraints

- Use the Chinese Ferrari perfume direction in new prompts.
- No cigarette.
- No secondary characters, no crowd.
- No visible bottle label or external brand logo.
- No neon, rain, or night in exterior frames.
- Red mode stays inside the elevator. Gray mode stays outside near the F40 or inside its cockpit.
