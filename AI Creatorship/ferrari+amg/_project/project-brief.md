# Project Brief — Ferrari + AMG

## Concept

A Ferrari 812 Superfast and a Mercedes-AMG GT3 race car in a head-to-head chase through an autumn European forest. Two philosophies of speed: Italian grand touring elegance vs German racing aggression. The forest is the arena — ancient, indifferent, swallowing both machines in mist and dappled light.

## Visual Contrast (Core Design Pillar)

This project is built on opposition:

| Ferrari 812 | AMG GT3 |
|---|---|
| Glossy metallic orange-red | Matte charcoal grey |
| Smooth, sculptural, reflective | Aggressive, angular, light-absorbing |
| Grand tourer — street car beauty | Race car — functional brutality |
| Warm hero color — pops in frame | Cool recessive tone — haunts the frame |

This contrast drives every shot decision. The Ferrari is always the warmer, more visible object. The AMG is always the darker presence.

## Time of Day

Late afternoon golden hour. Sun low in the west (15-20° above horizon), backlighting the cars in most chase angles. Light rakes through the forest canopy as diagonal god rays.

## Season

Autumn. Gold and amber deciduous foliage mixed with deep green conifers. Fallen leaves on road shoulders. Warm foliage color echoes the Ferrari's paint.

## Mood

Tense, cinematic, grounded. Not fantasy — this should feel like a real race captured by a real film crew. European automotive commercial energy, not video game energy. The forest has weight. The speed has consequence.
