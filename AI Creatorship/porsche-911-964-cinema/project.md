# Porsche 911 964 Cinema

## Brief

A 15-20 second cinematic storytelling film built around a white Porsche 911 964 Carrera. The car begins as a silent object inside a controlled orange studio, wakes through mechanical and electrical details, then breaks out as the studio dissolves into a real sunset road.

Core arc:

object at rest -> ignition -> restrained power -> studio becomes road -> tracking escape -> fade to black

## Art direction

The film starts with the same orange studio language as the reference board: warm cyclorama, glossy floor, sculptural stillness, controlled product lighting. As the car starts moving, the artificial studio surface becomes wet asphalt and the orange background opens into real scenery.

Tone: restrained, premium, mythic, mechanical. No driver reveal. The car is the character.

Runtime target: 15-20 seconds.

Visual continuity lock:

- White Porsche 911 964 Carrera coupe.
- Stock 964 proportions, round glass headlights, amber front indicators, black rubber bumper line.
- Silver five-spoke Cup wheels.
- Black rear engine grille and Carrera decklid script.
- Glossy white paint carrying warm orange studio reflections.

Primary transition idea:

The car does not simply drive from one place to another. Its movement creates the road. Tire friction turns the reflective studio floor into wet asphalt; the background dissolves into sunset scenery as the Porsche exits frame.
