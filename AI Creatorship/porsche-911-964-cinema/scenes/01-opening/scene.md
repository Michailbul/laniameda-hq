# The Set Opens

## Description

The film opens in a warm orange studio where the 911 964 sits in complete stillness. It wakes through details: electronics, headlight, engine, exhaust, tire friction. As it starts moving, the studio surface begins to fail. The glossy orange floor becomes wet asphalt. The orange background dissolves into sunset scenery. The Porsche drives out of the artificial set and exits frame into the real world.

## Storyboard

Target runtime: 15-20 seconds.

| Time | Shot | Beat | Camera |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0-2.5s | 001 | Studio calm. The Porsche is still, sculptural, almost too perfect. | Wide low studio frame, slow push-in |
| 2.5-5.0s | 002 | Interior electronics wake: gauges, dash lights, ignition life. | Close-up interior inserts |
| 5.0-7.0s | 003 | Headlights turn on; the car becomes aware. | Close-up on front headlight |
| 7.0-9.5s | 004 | Engine breath: exhaust pulse, body vibration, tail glow. | Low rear close-up |
| 9.5-12.0s | 005 | The car revs in place; tires scrub against glossy studio floor. | Low wheel macro |
| 12.0-16.0s | 006 | Acceleration begins; the studio dissolves into scenery. | Side tracking / rear three-quarter |
| 16.0-20.0s | 007 | Tracking escape into sunset; Porsche exits frame; fade to black. | Low rear tracking shot |

## Editorial Notes

- The first half should feel controlled and quiet.
- The transition begins only after tire friction and forward movement.
- The exterior does not need to appear instantly. Let the set dissolve in layers: floor first, background second, horizon last.
- Final image: sunset road, white 964 leaving frame, orange glow fading behind, black screen.
