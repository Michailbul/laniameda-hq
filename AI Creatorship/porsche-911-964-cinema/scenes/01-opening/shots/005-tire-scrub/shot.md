# Tire Scrub

## Intent

Show restrained power. The car is still partly trapped in the studio, but the tires are already fighting the floor.

## Direction

Macro low shot on the silver five-spoke Cup wheel and tire contact patch. The wheel starts rotating while the car holds for a beat. Rubber scrubs against the glossy orange floor, creating a faint dark contact mark and a small heat shimmer.

Camera: locked low macro, tire fills most of frame.

Light: orange floor reflection, crisp highlight on the wheel lip.

Physics: controlled tire friction, subtle rubber mark, no huge smoke cloud.
