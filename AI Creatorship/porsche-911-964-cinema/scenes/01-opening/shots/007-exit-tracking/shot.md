# Exit Tracking

## Intent

Release the Porsche from the studio and end on a clean cinematic exit.

## Direction

Low rear three-quarter tracking shot. The orange cyclorama is now mostly gone, replaced by a wet real road at sunset. The Porsche accelerates away, orange light fading behind it. The camera follows for a moment, then the car exits frame and the image fades to black.

Camera: low rear tracking, smooth and restrained.

Light: low sunset horizon, warm backlight, wet asphalt reflections trailing behind the car.

Action: the Porsche exits frame. Fade to black after the car leaves.
