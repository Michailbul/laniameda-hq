# Studio Calm

## Intent

Establish the Porsche as a silent object inside a controlled studio. This is the calm before ignition.

## Direction

Wide low angle inside a warm orange cyclorama studio. The white 911 964 sits perfectly still on a glossy reflective floor. Keep the frame clean and symmetrical enough to feel like a product shoot, with lots of negative space and a clear floor reflection.

Camera: slow push-in, almost imperceptible.

Light: warm orange studio wash, soft overhead strip reflections on the hood and roof, no harsh showroom glare.

Action: no car movement yet.
