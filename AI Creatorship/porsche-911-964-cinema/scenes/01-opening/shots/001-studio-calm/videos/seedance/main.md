# Seedance Sequence

Master sequence prompt for the 15-20 second film. Use as the base, then split into shorter generations if Seedance drifts.

## v1

One continuous sequence, built around one clear action per beat.

```
Use the attached Porsche 911 964 reference as the exact car identity lock.

Generate a 15-20 second cinematic automotive short film titled "The Set Opens".

Car identity must remain consistent: white Porsche 911 964 Carrera coupe, stock 964 proportions, round glass headlights, amber front indicators, black rubber bumper line, silver five-spoke Cup wheels, black rear engine grille, Carrera script on rear decklid, compact upright cabin, glossy white paint, no visible driver.

Shot 1, 0.0-2.5s: Wide low angle inside a warm orange cyclorama studio. The Porsche sits perfectly still on a glossy reflective floor. Slow push-in. No car movement yet.

Shot 2, 2.5-5.0s: Close-up interior inserts. The dashboard electronics wake up: gauge needles twitch, small amber instrument lights glow, ignition life appears inside the cabin. Keep the movement subtle and mechanical.

Shot 3, 5.0-7.0s: Close-up on the front round headlight. The headlight turns on with a controlled warm flare across the glass and white hood. The car feels awake.

Shot 4, 7.0-9.5s: Low rear close-up. The exhaust gives one visible pulse of vapor, the body vibrates slightly from the engine, red tail light glow appears on the glossy floor.

Shot 5, 9.5-12.0s: Macro wheel shot. The silver five-spoke Cup wheel starts rotating while the car holds for a beat; tire rubber scrubs against the glossy studio floor, creating a faint dark contact mark and a small shimmer of heat.

Shot 6, 12.0-16.0s: Side tracking shot as the Porsche starts accelerating. Under the moving tires, the orange reflective studio floor transforms into wet black asphalt. The transformation begins at the tire contact patch and spreads backward into the reflection.

Shot 7, 16.0-20.0s: Low rear tracking shot. The orange cyclorama background dissolves into a real sunset road with mist, road markings, and a low glowing horizon. The Porsche drives out through the dissolving studio light, exits frame to the right, and the image fades to black.

Style: hyperrealistic cinematic automotive film, controlled product lighting at the start, warm orange studio reflections, wet asphalt in the transition, low sunset backlight at the end, subtle film grain, realistic tire physics, restrained camera movement.

Avoid: visible driver, modern Porsche details, racing livery, exaggerated smoke, unreadable badge mutations, cartoon portal effects, sudden environment jump before the tires move.
```
