# Text Storyboard To Image

Storyboard prompt for generating a 3x3 visual board once the shot sequence is locked.

## v1

Use the attached 911 964 reference as the car identity source.

```
Create a cinematic 3x3 storyboard grid for a 15-20 second short film titled "The Set Opens", starring a white Porsche 911 964 Carrera coupe.

Keep the Porsche identity consistent in every panel: white Porsche 911 964 Carrera coupe, stock 964 body proportions, round glass headlights, amber front indicators, black rubber bumper line, silver five-spoke Cup wheels, black rear engine grille, Carrera script on rear decklid, compact upright cabin, glossy white paint, no visible driver, period-correct 1990s Porsche details.

Story arc: studio stillness -> ignition -> electronics wake -> headlights on -> engine breathing -> tires scrub the studio floor -> the studio dissolves into real scenery -> tracking escape into sunset -> fade to black.

Visual style: hyperrealistic cinematic automotive film, warm orange studio cyclorama, glossy reflective floor, controlled product lighting, then wet asphalt, cool mist, low sunset horizon, subtle film grain, premium editorial car campaign. Use black borders between panels. No captions, no labels, no watermark.

Panel 1: wide low shot in a warm orange studio, the white Porsche sits perfectly still on a glossy floor with a clean reflection.
Panel 2: close-up interior insert, Porsche gauges and dashboard electronics powering on in warm orange darkness.
Panel 3: close-up of the front round headlight turning on, small flare across the glass and white hood.
Panel 4: low rear close-up, exhaust gives a visible vapor pulse, red tail light glow begins.
Panel 5: macro wheel shot, silver five-spoke Cup wheel starts to rotate, tire scrubs against the glossy studio floor.
Panel 6: low side profile, the Porsche begins accelerating, studio floor under the tires starts turning into wet black asphalt.
Panel 7: rear three-quarter tracking shot, the orange cyclorama background dissolves into misty sunset scenery and road markings.
Panel 8: exterior road transition, the Porsche bursts out of the studio light onto a wet road at sunset, orange glow behind it.
Panel 9: final wide rear tracking frame, Porsche exits the edge of frame into the sunset road, long reflection trailing behind, fade-to-black feeling.
```
