# Set Dissolves

## Intent

Transform the studio into the real world through the car's motion.

## Direction

Side tracking shot as the Porsche accelerates across the studio. The transformation starts under the tires: glossy orange floor becomes wet black asphalt, then the reflection begins showing road markings and distant scenery. The orange background softens and dissolves into mist.

Camera: side profile tracking, low enough to see floor transformation.

Light: orange studio light mixing with cooler exterior mist.

Action: one forward acceleration. The environment changes because the car moves.
