# Engine Breath

## Intent

Turn electrical life into mechanical life.

## Direction

Low rear close-up on the 964's tail, exhaust, bumper line, and engine deck shape. The engine catches. The body gives a small vibration. One visible exhaust vapor pulse rolls into the warm studio air. Red tail light glow reflects on the floor.

Camera: low rear detail, slight handheld vibration or subtle lens tremor.

Light: orange studio light behind white paint, red tail light reflection on the glossy floor.

Physics: exhaust vapor expands once, then thins into the haze.
