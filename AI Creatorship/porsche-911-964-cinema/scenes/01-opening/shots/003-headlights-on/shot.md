# Headlights On

## Intent

Make the car feel aware. The headlight is treated like an eye opening.

## Direction

Close-up on the 964's round front headlight and white hood edge. The headlight turns on with a controlled warm flare. The orange studio reflection slides across the glass, then settles.

Camera: tight front detail, static or tiny push-in.

Light: headlight flare contained, no blown-out frame. Warm studio wash remains visible on the paint.

Action: one clean headlight ignition.
