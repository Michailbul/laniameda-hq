# Electronics Wake

## Intent

Show the first sign of life from inside the car before external motion begins.

## Direction

Close-up interior inserts inside the Porsche cabin. Dashboard electronics power on: gauge needles twitch, amber instrument lights glow, small reflections move across black plastic and glass. Keep it tactile and period-correct, not modern digital.

Camera: macro interior close-ups, shallow depth of field, slow rack focus from dark gauge glass to glowing instruments.

Light: low warm orange spill from the studio, amber dashboard glow.

Action: electronics wake only. No driving yet.
