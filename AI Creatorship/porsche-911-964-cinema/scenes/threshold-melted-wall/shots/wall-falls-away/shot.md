# Wall Falls Away

## Intent

Show that the set itself is failing, not simply opening like a door.

## Direction

Hold on the threshold while the curved orange studio wall edge softens, recedes, and visually melts away into mist and outdoor depth. The Porsche remains planted in frame, idling, while the opening grows wider and the world outside becomes more dominant.

Camera: mostly static, minimal drift to preserve the effect.

Light: orange light clinging to the remaining wall edge, natural sky light and fog spilling inward.

Action: the wall changes shape; the Porsche does not perform a second action here.
