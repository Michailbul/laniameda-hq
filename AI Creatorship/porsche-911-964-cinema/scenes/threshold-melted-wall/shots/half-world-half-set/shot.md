# Half World Half Set

## Intent

Capture the signature still image of the sequence: the Porsche living between two worlds at once.

## Direction

Rear or side-biased three-quarter composition where the Porsche sits with the orange studio floor and reflections still visible beneath it, while the wet real road and misty exterior dominate the space ahead. This frame should feel balanced and graphic: one half controlled studio, one half natural atmosphere.

Camera: composed, almost photographic, with a slight sense of held breath.

Light: warm orange rim and floor reflection from the studio, cool gray-blue mist and sunset highlights from outside.

Action: almost no movement; prioritize composition and tension.
