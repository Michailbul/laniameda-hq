# Threshold Reveal

## Intent

Introduce the new reality: the Porsche is on, idling, and facing an opening where the orange studio wall has dissolved into a real road outside.

## Direction

Low rear three-quarter angle on the white 911 964 inside the orange studio. A large curved section of the wall is gone, revealing a wet road, pine silhouettes, mountain haze, and sunset sky outside. The car remains mostly still, engine alive, with subtle tail light presence and a glossy reflection on the floor.

Camera: restrained slow push or very subtle dolly.

Light: warm orange studio spill from behind and above, cooler natural exterior light entering through the opening, wet reflections joining both worlds.

Action: the car idles. The shot is about the reveal, not yet the departure.
