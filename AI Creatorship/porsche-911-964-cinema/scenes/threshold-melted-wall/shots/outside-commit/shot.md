# Outside Commit

## Intent

Show the exact moment the Porsche commits to leaving the artificial set and moving into the world outside.

## Direction

Low tracking rear three-quarter shot as the Porsche starts rolling forward across the threshold. Wet road texture takes over fully beneath the wheels. The remaining orange set glow falls behind the car while sunset mist and mountain road depth open ahead.

Camera: smooth low tracking movement following the rear quarter.

Light: wet asphalt reflections, warm orange glow behind, cool dusk air ahead.

Action: one clean forward move into the exterior world.
