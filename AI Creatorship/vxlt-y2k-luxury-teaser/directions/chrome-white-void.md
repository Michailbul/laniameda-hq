# Chrome White Void

## Thesis

An expensive, withheld Y2K luxury teaser: white void, black panther, reflective silver products, and a calm adult Japanese woman styled as a futuristic fashion figure. The image should feel like a contact sheet from a luxury campaign film, not a poster.

## Visual Grammar

- Pure white seamless studio with soft ground shadows and polished white reflections.
- Monochrome palette with silver as the only accent.
- Sculptural product moments integrated into the storyboard, not pasted on top.
- Strong negative space and controlled symmetry.
- The panther supplies primal tension; the woman supplies restraint.

## Avoid

- Cyberpunk neon.
- Black studio backgrounds.
- Decorative gradients.
- Gold, red, beige, or colorful accents.
- Flat typography for `VXLT`; it must feel like an object.
