# Teaser Collage

## Description

Non-linear luxury teaser storyboard for `VXLT`: a sequence of controlled editorial frames inside a seamless white void. The sequence alternates between logo, subject, panther, metallic fabric, product still life, reflections, and distant final tableau.

## Storyboard

1. `VXLT` liquid-metal title object with woman and panther blurred behind it.
2. Woman standing alone in white void with a soft shadow.
3. Black panther walking slowly beside her.
4. Close-up of reflective silver fabric catching light.
5. Metallic handbag on glossy white surface.
6. Panther eyes in sharp focus against the white environment.
7. Woman subtly interacting with the panther.
8. Abstract hair or fabric motion with precise movement.
9. Silver accessory still life arranged like sculpture.
10. Reflections of both subjects on polished white surfaces.
11. Dynamic motion-blur frame suggesting movement through space.
12. Final distant frame: both subjects still, untouchable, fading into white.
