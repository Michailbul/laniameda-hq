# Initial Storyboard Collage

First Nano Banana Pro prompt for the full 12-frame `VXLT` luxury teaser collage.

## v1

Structured prompt optimized for exact frame count, readable title typography, and consistent monochrome chrome-white art direction.

```
GOAL:
Create one image: a clean 12-frame luxury teaser storyboard collage for the fictional brand "VXLT", inspired by refined Y2K fashion-film aesthetics.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
One complete collage image arranged as exactly 12 separate frames in a clean structured grid.
Use a 4 columns x 3 rows grid.
Each frame must have thin white gutters and a precise editorial contact-sheet layout.
Every frame must look like a still from the same high-end teaser film.

TEXT:
Render the exact word "VXLT" only once, in Frame 1.
No other readable text anywhere.
Frame 1: "VXLT" is the central visual object, not flat typography. Make it a physical liquid-metal chrome Y2K logo form with smooth sculptural distortions, reflective silver highlights, and fluid rounded edges. It should feel cast from polished mercury or chrome, placed in the studio space. Behind it, softly out of focus, place the adult Japanese woman and the black panther inside the white void for depth, but keep the logo dominant.

SETTING:
Pure white seamless infinite studio space with no visible walls, no horizon line, no set edges, no architectural boundaries.
Use glossy white floor moments only where reflections are needed.
High-key studio lighting: large soft overhead and side sources, clean shadows, strong chrome highlights, controlled highlight rolloff.

SUBJECTS:
Adult Japanese woman in her mid-20s, calm controlled expression, natural soft feminine silhouette, sleek futuristic high-fashion styling, reflective silver metallic fabric, minimal sculptural forms, composed luxury presence.
Realistic black panther, calm controlled posture, highly detailed black fur texture, sharp reflective eyes, primal power held in restraint, elegant movement.
The woman and panther must feel calm, controlled, slightly surreal, and expensive. No aggression, no fantasy styling, no cartoon look.

FRAME PLAN - EXACTLY THESE 12 FRAMES:
1. Strong title cut: physical liquid-metal chrome "VXLT" logo centered; woman and black panther softly out of focus in the white studio background.
2. Full-body fashion frame: the woman standing alone in a vast seamless white void, soft precise shadow beneath her, silver futuristic garment catching light.
3. Panther movement frame: the black panther walking slowly beside her, realistic fur texture, elegant controlled gait, white void around them.
4. Material insert: close-up macro of reflective silver fabric catching high-key studio light, liquid highlights, crisp folds.
5. Product still: sculptural metallic handbag placed on a glossy white surface with mirror-like reflection, minimal composition.
6. Panther eye close-up: the panther's eyes in sharp focus, black fur detail visible, white environment reflected in the eyes.
7. Controlled interaction: the woman subtly touches or gestures near the panther with composed restraint; no dramatic action.
8. Abstract motion: silver fabric or black hair moving with precision, caught mid-motion, clean motion direction, no chaos.
9. Product sculpture: silver accessories arranged like a small luxury sculpture on white, crisp shadows, chrome and brushed metal textures.
10. Reflection frame: reflections of both the woman and panther on a polished white surface, abstracted but legible.
11. Dynamic passage: slight cinematic motion blur suggesting the woman and panther moving through white space, one clear direction of motion.
12. Final tableau: both standing still in the far distance, almost untouchable, fading softly into the white void.

VISUAL STYLE:
Ultra-clean high-fashion editorial meets futuristic luxury cinema.
Monochrome palette: black, white, gray, and dominant reflective silver only.
Strong emphasis on material, texture, negative space, symmetry, and sculptural framing.
Y2K-inspired luxury styling through chrome, liquid metal, reflective fabrics, sleek silhouettes, and minimal bold forms.

CAMERA:
Medium-format fashion photography and cinema hybrid.
Sharp focus where intended, controlled depth of field, refined lens compression, no noise.
Mix wide full-body frames, macro material inserts, product still lifes, and close cinematic details.

CONSTRAINTS:
Exactly 12 frames, no missing frames, no extra frames.
No watermark, no captions, no subtitles, no logos except the single "VXLT" title in Frame 1.
No neon, no colorful accents, no gold, no beige, no warm palette.
No visible studio walls, corners, seams, props, stands, cables, or set equipment.
Do not sexualize the woman. Keep the styling luxury-fashion, controlled, and editorial.
Do not make the panther aggressive, snarling, mythical, or cartoonish.

VALIDATION:
Before finalizing, check that the collage has exactly 12 distinct panels in a 4x3 grid, that "VXLT" appears only once and is readable in Frame 1, and that every frame belongs to the same monochrome chrome-white luxury teaser world.
```
