# 12-Frame VXLT Storyboard Collage

## Intent

Generate the first key visual for the project: one 12-frame grid that can serve as the campaign storyboard, still-image reference sheet, and downstream video direction board.

## Direction

Keep the grid clean and editorial. Each frame should read as a distinct luxury film still, with consistent white studio physics, silver material behavior, and controlled contrast between the woman and the panther.
