# VXLT Y2K Luxury Teaser

## Brief

Create a 12-frame collage styled like a high-end luxury teaser film for the fictional brand `VXLT`.

The deliverable is a monochrome, silver-accented storyboard image: futuristic, minimal, sharply refined, with a young adult Japanese woman and a black panther staged in an infinite white studio void.

## Art Direction

- Format: one structured 12-frame grid, like a fashion teaser storyboard.
- Tone: cold luxury, control, futuristic elegance, quiet dominance.
- World: pure seamless white studio space with no visible boundaries.
- Color: black, white, gray, dominant reflective silver; no warm color accents.
- Lighting: high-key studio light, large soft sources, precise shadows, sharp chrome highlights.
- Camera: medium-format fashion photography crossed with luxury cinema stills.
- Texture priority: chrome, reflective metallic fabric, glossy white surfaces, realistic black panther fur.
- Typography: `VXLT` appears only in the title frame, as a physical liquid-metal Y2K chrome object.

## Hard Constraints

- Exactly 12 frames.
- No watermark.
- No extra text beyond `VXLT`.
- Keep the woman adult, calm, composed, high-fashion, and non-sexualized.
- The panther must read as realistic and controlled, not fantasy or cartoon.
