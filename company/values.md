# Values

_How we work. What we stand for. Non-negotiable._

---

## Core Values

### 1. Insane Attention to Detail
The frame, the color, the cut, the word — everything is intentional. Nothing ships that we're not proud of. Details are where character lives.

### 2. Never Settle for Mediocrity
Good enough isn't. We push until the work is genuinely extraordinary — or we go back to the start. Mediocre output with an AI stamp is worse than no output.

### 3. Begin With the End in Mind
We don't start with tools or formats. We start with the **feeling** we want to create, then reverse-engineer everything to get there. Emotion first. Execution second.

### 4. Approach Everything as Art
Every brief is a creative problem. Every deliverable is an artifact. We bring the same care to a 30-second reel as a short film — and the same care to an automation workflow as a piece of code poetry.

### 5. AI is the Instrument, Not the Shortcut
We don't use AI to avoid craft. We use AI to unlock what wouldn't be possible otherwise. The human eye, the human judgment, the human taste — always in the loop.

### 6. Marketing First
Every product decision has a content angle. Before we build, we ask: how does this ship? What's the story? Who's it for? Content isn't afterthought — it's part of the product.

### 7. Ship, Then Refine
We move. We validate. We compound. Perfect is the enemy of shipped. But shipped mediocrity is worse than waiting. Find the balance: ship the 80% that matters, then iterate.

## Working Principles

**For agents and collaborators:**
- Michael approves and ships. Agents draft, build, prepare — never auto-publish
- Work from real files and context — never fabricate about the studio or codebase
- Private context stays private — don't leak USER.md, MEMORY.md, or credentials
- Specifics over inspiration — concrete examples beat vague motivation, always
- Route to the right agent/person — don't duplicate effort
