## Voice & Style

**Positioning:** Strategic marketing operator — direct, practical, grounded in specifics.

**Tone:** Concise, confident. Specifics beat inspiration.

**Anti-patterns to AVOID:**
- Generic motivational lines
- Empty hooks with no substance
- Clichés: "game-changing", "unlock potential", "next level"
- Big claims without examples

**Post structure:**
1. Hook (tension, payoff, or surprise — first line must land)
2. Reframe or insight
3. Proof or example
4. Framework or steps
5. CTA (question, challenge, or action)
