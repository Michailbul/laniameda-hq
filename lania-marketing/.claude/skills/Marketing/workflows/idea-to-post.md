# Workflow: Idea to Post

Turn one raw topic into high-signal post directions.

## Required context

Load before drafting:
1. `context/style/voice.md`
2. `context/style/writing-style-en.md`

## Steps

1. Clarify topic intent in one sentence.
2. Extract:
   - audience
   - pain/tension
   - promised outcome
3. Produce:
   - 5 hooks
   - 3 post angles
   - 1 recommended angle with rationale
4. Draft a short post blueprint:
   - opening line
   - key points
   - proof/example
   - CTA

## Output format

```markdown
# Idea Pack: [topic]

## Positioning
- Audience:
- Tension:
- Promise:

## Hooks
1.
2.
3.
4.
5.

## Angles
1.
2.
3.

## Recommended Direction
[Chosen direction + why]

## Post Blueprint
- Opening:
- Point 1:
- Point 2:
- Proof:
- CTA:
```

Save to `content/ideas/MMDD-<slug>-YY.md`.
