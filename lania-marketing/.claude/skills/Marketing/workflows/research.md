# Workflow: Topic Research

Collect high-signal inputs before writing copy.

## Required context

Load before researching:
1. `context/style/voice.md`
2. `context/style/writing-style-en.md`

## Inputs

- Topic (`$ARGUMENTS`)
- Optional audience and platform constraints

## Research goals

1. Identify what is trending now around the topic.
2. Extract 5-10 concrete examples, data points, or cases.
3. Find strong contrarian or underused angles.
4. Build a short evidence-backed idea bank for writing.

## Process

1. Topic map:
   - key claims
   - common narratives
   - controversies
2. Source scan:
   - authoritative links
   - recent discussions
   - practical examples
3. Angle extraction:
   - angle
   - why it matters
   - suggested hook
4. Output pack for writing workflows.

## Output format

```markdown
# Research Pack: [topic]

## Topic map
- Common narrative:
- Contrarian narrative:
- Risk of generic take:

## Evidence bank
1. [data point/example + source]
2. [data point/example + source]

## Content angles
1. Angle:
   - Why this works:
   - Hook:
2. Angle:
   - Why this works:
   - Hook:

## Recommended angle
[one selected direction + rationale]
```

Save to `content/ideas/MMDD-research-<slug>-YY.md`.
