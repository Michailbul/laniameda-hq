## Post 1 — The queue is the discipline

Most creators treat the content queue as a to-do list.

It's not.

It's a buffer between your work and the algorithm.

When the queue is full:
→ you post from strength, not desperation
→ you don't write at 11pm because something "needs to go out"
→ the content is better because it had 24h to breathe

Minimum viable queue: 3 posts ahead.
That's it. That's the whole system.

## Post 2 — The capture is the hardest part (it's not)

People say: "I don't have time to create content."

What they mean: "I haven't built the capture habit."

Capture takes 30 seconds:
- One line of what you just built
- One thing that surprised you
- One thing that broke (and how you fixed it)

That's 3 posts.

You're not low on content.
You're low on capture frequency.
