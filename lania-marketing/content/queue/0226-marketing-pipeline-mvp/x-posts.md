# X Posts (5) — Marketing Pipeline MVP (no video)

## Post 1 — Overwhelm → operating system
You’re not inconsistent.
You just don’t have a system.

If you’re building + learning every day but posting nothing, it’s because your work isn’t being converted into *content-ready atoms*.

My loop now:
Capture → Distill → Draft → Approve → Post → Archive.

One 60-second capture/day is enough.

## Post 2 — “No video” is not an excuse
No video? Good.

Text + carousels are the fastest way to build signal *without* production debt.

MVP stack:
- 1 X post/day (one idea)
- 2 LinkedIn posts/week (narrative + takeaway)
- 1 carousel/week (templated)

Consistency beats cinematic.

## Post 3 — Taste-driven automation
Most “AI automation” content is: screenshots + buzzwords.

We’re building something else:
**taste-driven systems**.

Automation that:
- ships reliably
- reads like a human wrote it
- looks intentional (constraints > decoration)

Art + engineering + philosophy — or it’s just scripts.

## Post 4 — The only input you need (daily)
Daily content doesn’t require daily writing.

It requires daily *capture*.

Template:
RAW: what happened today?
ANGLE: what’s the tension/surprise?
PROOF: screenshot/snippet/before-after

Send that to your “marketing brain.”
They’ll turn it into drafts.

## Post 5 — Carousels without design hell
Carousels fail when every carousel becomes a design project.

Rule:
**Carousels are templated. Always.**

1080×1350
2 fonts
1 accent color
7–9 slides
progress indicator

The content carries.
The design stays consistent.
