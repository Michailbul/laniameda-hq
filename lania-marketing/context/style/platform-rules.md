# Platform Rules

## X / Twitter

- Strong one-line hook first
- Prefer 3-10 tweet threads for educational content
- Keep each tweet one main idea

## LinkedIn

- Narrative or lesson-driven
- Clear professional takeaway
- Slightly longer body than X

## Instagram caption

- Emotional opening + concise insight
- Short paragraphs
- CTA aligned with carousel

## Newsletter snippet

- Keep highly specific
- Promise clear takeaway in first 2 lines
- Invite full-read click intent
