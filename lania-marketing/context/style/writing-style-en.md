# Writing Style (English)

## Universal rules

1. First line must carry tension, payoff, or surprise.
2. Keep lines short and scannable.
3. Teach a mechanism, not only a conclusion.
4. Include at least one concrete example.
5. End with momentum (question, challenge, or action).

## Post structure

1. Hook
2. Reframe or insight
3. Proof or example
4. Framework or steps
5. CTA

## Clarity checks

- Remove filler words.
- Remove abstractions without examples.
- Replace broad claims with specific outcomes.
