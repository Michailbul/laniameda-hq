### SHOT 1 (00:00-00:01.5) — Low Front Attack
- **EFFECT:** Low front tracking + foreground floral streaking

- The shot opens with immediate high-speed momentum and aggressive cinematic presence

- The camera stays smooth but urgent, giving a sense of expensive speed rather than chaos

- The road geometry becomes a key visual element, making the car feel embedded in a designed landscape
- Motion remains fast and fluid, with the car cutting a clean line through the frame
- This becomes the environmental showcase before dropping back into intimacy

### SHOT 4 (00:04.5-00:06) — Rear Chase Surge

- The rear bodywork stays dominant while flowers streak hard in the foreground and subtle petals lift in the slipstream
- A controlled acceleration ramp gives the shot a forceful push without breaking realism
- This is the **SIGNATURE VISUAL EFFECT** moment, where the car feels fastest and most alive

### SHOT 5 (00:06-00:08) — Hero Scenic Resolve
- **EFFECT:** Front-quarter hero glide + scenic expansion
- The camera swings around into a clean front three-quarter hero glide, revealing the mountain road, pink fields, and the car in one premium composition
- Motion is still fast, but the energy becomes more elegant and composed for the ending beat
- Reflections move across the hood and windshield while the environment opens behind it
- The video lands with a polished high-end automotive commercial finish
