# Seedance Prompt — Cinematic Flyover Car Showcase

## Section 1: SHOT-BY-SHOT EFFECTS TIMELINE

### SHOT 1 (00:00-00:02) — High Reveal Approach
- **EFFECT:** Overhead glide-in + floral parallax sweep
- The camera begins slightly above and behind the red exotic sports car as it drives through a winding mountain road surrounded by dense pink flowers
- The move starts in a high rear angle, letting the road geometry and flower fields establish the environment
- The flowers create soft flowing parallax beneath the frame while the car remains the visual anchor
- The shot transitions smoothly into a descending camera move toward the roofline

### SHOT 2 (00:02-00:04) — Roofline Flyover
- **EFFECT:** Descending camera flyover + reflective surface sweep
- The camera glides directly over the top of the moving car in one clean cinematic move, passing from rear roofline toward the windshield and hood
- Reflections of the sky, flowers, and surrounding landscape sweep across the glossy red bodywork as the camera crosses overhead
- The move should feel smooth, expensive, and intentional, not like a drone survey shot
- The flyover creates the central luxury showcase moment and carries momentum into the front reveal

### SHOT 3 (00:04-00:06) — Front Quarter Drop
- **EFFECT:** Arc-down reveal + speed-maintained tracking
- After passing over the roof, the camera drops into a front three-quarter angle while continuing to move with the car through the curve
- The motion stays fluid and dynamic, with the camera wrapping around the front quarter in a graceful arc
- The flowers blur softly in the lower frame while the hood, headlights, and front body lines are shown in premium cinematic detail
- This is the **SIGNATURE VISUAL EFFECT** moment, where the camera choreography itself becomes the spectacle

### SHOT 4 (00:06-00:08) — Hero Glide Exit
- **EFFECT:** Lateral drift resolve + wide scenic expansion
- The camera eases outward from the car into a slightly wider front-side hero glide, revealing the road, flower field, and mountains together
- Motion remains smooth and elegant, with no abrupt cut or jitter
- The car feels dominant and luxurious while the environment expands around it
- The shot resolves as a polished high-end automotive commercial finish
