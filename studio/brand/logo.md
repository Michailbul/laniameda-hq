## Approved Backgrounds

The logo/wordmark may appear on:
- Obsidian `#0A0805`
- Parchment `#FAF7F4`
- Photography with sufficient contrast
- Any gradient from the approved gradient list

Never place on busy patterns or low-contrast surfaces.
