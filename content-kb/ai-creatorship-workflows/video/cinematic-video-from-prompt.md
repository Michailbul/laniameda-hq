# Cinematic Video from Text Prompt

> Generate high-quality cinematic video clips from a text description — for storytelling, brand content, or social.

## Best workflow right now

Not yet tested by studio as a dedicated workflow.

From model research (March 2026), the current frontier tools are:
- **Kling 3.0** — multi-shot storyboarding (up to 6 camera cuts), 15s clips, 4K, custom duration
- **LTX-2 (Lightricks)** — first open-weights audio+video model, native 4K, self-hostable
- **Seedance 2.0 (ByteDance)** — hyperrealistic video, noted as "Hollywood panic" level quality
