## COLOR SYSTEM FOR THIS PROJECT

- **Car paint:** Rosso orange-red — warm, not cool. Reads amber in golden hour. Deep burgundy under trees.
- **Environment palette:** Alpine spring — pine green, pink wildflower fields, pale blue sky, soft forest dappled light
- **Contrast logic:** Car warmth (red/amber) against cool environment (green/blue/grey) — natural tension, no grading needed
- **Studio shots:** White/grey neutral floor, soft top-down key light, single shadow pool
